Gallery

Explore our mouthwatering gallery of culinary delights. Immerse yourself in a visual feast of beautifully crafted plates that will leave you craving for more. Experience the artistry and flavors that make our dishes truly unforgettable. Buon appetito!

Indulge in our captivating Chicken Carciofi with Piccata Sauce. Tender chicken and artichoke hearts harmonize with a tangy blend of capers, white wine, and lemon. This exquisite plate offers a symphony of flavors that will leave a lasting impression!

Experience the divine flavors of our Italian Shrimp Gratin. Succulent shrimp, coated in crispy breadcrumbs and topped with melted cheese, rest delicately on a bed of fresh arugula. This dish is a true indulgence for the senses.

On the right, Chicken Parmesan with Orecchiette in a luscious Mozzarella and Tomato Sauce. And on the left, Pinsa with Prosciutto San Daniele, Arugula, Tomato, and Mozzarella.
These exquisite dishes epitomize our culinary mastery, inviting you to indulge in a memorable dining experience.

On a pristine white plate, tender grilled octopus with smoky grill marks steals the spotlight. Alongside, velvety mashed potatoes provide a comforting contrast. Garnished with vibrant herbs, this visually stunning and harmoniously flavorful dish is a true masterpiece for your gallery.
