About the Restaurant

Welcome to Maestrale Restaurant, an exquisite culinary destination located in the heart of Florida. Our restaurant is not just a place to dine; it's a celebration of Italian culture, tradition, and flavors.

At Maestrale, we are delighted to be a family-owned restaurant with a heartwarming connection to Puglia, Italy. Our journey started over 50 years ago when our family set out on a gastronomic adventure, sharing the authentic flavors of Italy with the world. This cherished heritage fills our restaurant with a welcoming atmosphere of warmth and familiarity.

We take great pride in our culinary expertise, especially in our handpicked collection of fine wines from all over Italy. Each bottle is carefully chosen to perfectly complement our exquisite dishes, ensuring a truly unforgettable dining experience for our treasured guests. Join us at Maestrale as we celebrate the beauty of Italy's traditions and flavors, making every visit a memorable one!

Having worked in the restaurant industry for over five decades, we have honed our craft to perfection, consistently delivering unparalleled service and flavors that leave a lasting impression on all who visit us. It is our passion for authentic Italian cuisine that drives us to continually innovate and present a menu that pays homage to the vibrant diversity of Italy's culinary tapestry.

Whether you are indulging in our signature pasta dishes, savoring the aromatic wood-fired pizzas, or relishing the freshest seafood creations, each plate is crafted with love and an unwavering commitment to quality. Our talented chefs pour their heart into every creation, ensuring that every bite at Maestrale is a celebration of flavor and tradition.

We invite you to join us on this gastronomic voyage, where each dish tells a story, and each sip of wine evokes the essence of Italy. As we share the treasures of Puglia and beyond, we hope to create an unforgettable dining experience that leaves you yearning for more.

From our family to yours, welcome to Maestrale Restaurant a true taste of Italy in the heart of Florida.
